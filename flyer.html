About the Event

Ilm Nexus is a youth-led digital skills and career empowerment hub that equips students with practical digital skills to prepare them for the future of work.

After successful editions at KWASU and FSS Oyo, we're proud to bring the 3rd edition to UniIlorin. This conference will feature hands-on workshops, expert talks, and networking opportunities with industry professionals.
